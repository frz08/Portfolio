ABOUT ME

I'm Farhaz Kolathoor — a Sales, Business Development, and Data Analytics professional with international experience across India, Indonesia, and Canada. My career spans hospitality, retail, international trade, tourism, and supply chain industries.

I began in sales and business development, working with Zomato where I exceeded targets and won a sales competition. I later moved to Indonesia, gaining hands-on experience in the export-import timber industry — building deep intuition for international trade and supply chain operations that now directly informs my analytics work.

As Head of Sales & Marketing at Apollo Sindoori Hotels, I helped launch and manage multiple brands end-to-end — from a fine dining restaurant and cloud kitchen to an advertising agency and a record label. That experience shaped how I think about data: not just as numbers, but as the story behind every business decision.

Currently based in Calgary, Canada, I'm studying Supply Chain Management at SAIT while expanding into Data Analytics, Microsoft Fabric, Power BI, and AI-powered application development — building real tools with Claude Sonnet, Streamlit & Claude Code.
